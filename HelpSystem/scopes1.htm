Scopes

  

The scopes page is where you can store the details of any project that you are scoping and quoting for, but have not begun yet. The purpose of the Scopes page is to allow for easy and quick transition when a scope becomes a job, and to save you entering information more times than you need to.

All the processes for adding, editing and removing information are identical to the Projects Page. 

When a scope becomes a working project, simply alter the job status to New Project using the drop down menu. The scope will be relocated to the Projects Page.
