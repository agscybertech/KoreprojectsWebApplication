User Settings

  


This is where you enter your details and upload your logo that will act as the public profile for your business. This information is available to any of your contacts. It allows others to quickly and easily source details such as your business address, phone number and email addresses. Fill in the form, giving whatever details you want your Kore Project contacts to have access to.





All these details will also be used to customize your screens and printouts.
