Manage Worksheet Items

  

You can manage jobs which have been approved or are pending approval by clicking EDIT in the Action column.

When work has not yet been approved it will appear in a table entitled ‘Work Pending Approval’. By clicking ‘Approve’ in the action column, you will switch the job from the ‘Pending Approval’ table to the ‘Approved’ table. To transfer work from the ‘Approved’ table to the ‘Pending Approval’ table, click Disapprove in the Action column.
